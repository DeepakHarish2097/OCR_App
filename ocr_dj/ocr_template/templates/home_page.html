Welcome to Ocr App


OCR - Optical character recognition or optical character reader is the electronic or mechanical conversion of images of typed or printed text into machine-encoded text, whether from a scanned document or a photo of a document.

Widely used as a form of data entry from printed paper data records – whether passport documents, invoices, or any suitable documentation – it is a common method of digitizing printed texts.
